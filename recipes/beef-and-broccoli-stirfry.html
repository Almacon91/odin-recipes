Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, and minced garlic.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, stir-fry the beef until browned.
  3. Add the broccoli florets and continue stir-frying until tender-crisp.
  4. Pour the sauce over the beef and broccoli.
  5. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring to coat the ingredients evenly. Serve over rice or noodles.
