One-Pot Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine spaghetti, water, cherry tomatoes, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat and cook, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is al dente and most of the water has evaporated.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in fresh basil. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  4. Serve immediately.
