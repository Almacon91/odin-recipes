  • 4 bell peppers (any color you prefer)
  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut off the tops of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or turkey until browned. Drain any excess fat. Add the taco seasoning and prepare according to the packet instructions.
  4. Stir in the cooked rice.
  5. Stuff each bell pepper with the meat and rice mixture. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
