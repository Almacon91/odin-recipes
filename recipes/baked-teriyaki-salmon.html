Baked Teriyaki Salmon: 4 salmon fillets 1/4 cup soy sauce 2 tablespoons honey 1 tablespoon rice vinegar Sesame seeds for garnish (optional) Instructions: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, and rice vinegar. Place the salmon fillets in a baking dish and pour the teriyaki sauce over them. Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
