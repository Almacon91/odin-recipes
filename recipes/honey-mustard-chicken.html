Honey Mustard Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and pour the honey mustard mixture over them.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
